Captain's Party

The Captain's Party was a great way to kick off the weekend’s festivities. We held a large raffle for some great prizes including items such as fishing rods and tackle, Budweiser gear, grilling accessories, jewelry and an inflatable kayak!

The B.U.G.S. tournament was about increasing the public’s awareness about sepsis. We talked about how your contributions to the Erin Kay Flatley Foundation are making that happen. We encouraged everyone to come out and have fun! Anglers, sponsors, friends, family - all were welcome!
